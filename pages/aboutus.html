About us

Texas Choral Riser Rental provides live event seating risers all over the United States. From choral risers, photos risers, and innovative and custom audience seating and tent flooring solutions, we provide exciting and high impact riser solutions to our clients and their event attendees. Our company continues to grow, and we would love for you to be our next client.

Our Experience in
Live Events

What makes the crew at Texas Choral Riser Rental the best choice for audience seating risers? One answer – our experience. Through our parent company, In Depth Events, we’ve provided live event services for thousands of events, with millions of attendees.
With the largest staging inventory in the State of Texas (and one of the largest in the country), innovative design ideas, and a long list of satisfied clients, we provide the best possible experience for our clients. Our audience seating risers, choral risers, commencement risers, and tent flooring solutions offer our clients the ability to provide comfortable and safe seating for their event attendees.
In addition, our photo riser solutions and our press riser solutions provide specialty risers for photographers and videographers alike. With our rental risers, you’ll never miss a moment of the action at events ranging from high school graduations to professional sporting events.
