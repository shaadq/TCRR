Applications for Choral Risers and Choir Risers

Choral risers and choir risers have become indispensable assets in countless venues, from houses of worship and performance halls to schools, malls, and outdoor stadiums. By placing performers on multi-level stages and prioritizing sightlines, these structures enhance both the visual and auditory aspects of choral performances, ensuring that each member is clearly seen and heard.
Customization is key when selecting the appropriate choral riser for your event. Our risers are available in various styles, shapes, and sizes to accommodate standing or seated arrangements, providing flexibility for both organizers and performers. Choir risers can even be built according to the dimensions of your specific venue, ensuring seamless integration with your event's existing layout and space.
With extensive experience providing choral riser rentals to elementary schools, collegiate institutions, as well as professional organizations and houses of worship, Texas Choral Riser Rental's expertise is trusted throughout the state of Texas and nationwide. When you choose our company for your needs, you will get the perfect combination of safety and professionalism.

Maintaining a Safe Event Environment built?

At Texas Choral Riser Rental, we prioritize safety above all else. Our choral and choir riser systems are built using Bil-Jax 4’x4' steel staging panels, selected for their strength and durability. Panels are supported by steel legs measured to the desired heights, and each structure is leveled, locked together, and cross-braced for maximum stability.
Our commitment to safety includes adherence to various guidelines and specifications:
  • Compliance with ADA guidelines
  • Incorporation of back rails, side rails, and steps between rows
  • Consideration of load tolerances and municipality codes
  • Knowledge of height and width requirements for specific structures
With these and other measures in place, our clients can rest assured that their event will meet all necessary safety requirements while remaining visually and acoustically engaging for audiences.
