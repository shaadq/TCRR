What are Commencement Risers ?

Commencement and Graduation Risers serve two critical purposes at any graduation event. They either lift deans, instructors, and speakers for a better view, or position the audience higher for an easier view of the event. In either case, these seating risers are specifically designed to support a live load, indicating their preparedness to withstand movement during entry and exit. They are constructed using 4×4 Bil-Jax staging material, steel legs, and additional support from cross braces for outstanding stability, even on uneven terrains.
If the client is using the risers as a stage for dignitaries and for graduates to “walk across the stage,” they are designed with ADA guidelines in mind. We use ADA ramps to make sure students get up and down to the podium safely! Many times, the stage is built on a sports field so that the sports seating can be used for audiences. On the other hand, if the client is using risers for audience seating, then the risers offer elevated sitting positions for a clear view of the ceremony.
Whether indoors or outdoors, on football fields, soccer fields, or gym floors, our risers remain steadfast, expanding your options for ceremony locations. Our commencement and graduation risers embody absolute safety and functionality, exuding an air of sophistication to match your ceremony's importance, regardless of the location.
